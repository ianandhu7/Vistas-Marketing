# v-learning spring application

## Description

This web application that provides an online learning platform called Vista's Learning. It allows students to learn at their own pace and convenience, with an exciting blend of live classes, concept videos, practice modules, quizzes, revision tests, and other interactive e-learning materials.

## Features

- **Live Classes:** Engage in real-time interactive sessions with experienced instructors who guide you through the curriculum, answer questions, and provide personalized support.

- **Animated Videos:** Access a library of concept videos that simplify complex topics and enhance understanding. These videos cover a wide range of subjects and are designed to make learning engaging and enjoyable.

- **Practice Modules:** Reinforce your learning through interactive practice modules that allow you to apply concepts and test your understanding. These modules offer immediate feedback and help identify areas for improvement.

- **Quizzes:** Assess your knowledge with quizzes that cover different topics and difficulty levels. These quizzes are designed to challenge you and reinforce learning while providing valuable insights into your progress.

- **Revision Tests:** Prepare for exams with comprehensive revision tests that cover the entire curriculum. These tests help you review and consolidate your knowledge, ensuring you are well-prepared for assessments.

- **E-Learning Materials:** Access a wealth of additional e-learning materials, such as study guides, reference materials, and supplementary resources. These materials complement the curriculum and provide further opportunities for exploration and learning.

## Technologies Used
